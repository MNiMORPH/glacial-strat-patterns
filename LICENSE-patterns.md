# Licence for the pattern assets

The fill-pattern assets in this repository — the SVG and PNG tiles under `svg/`
and `png/`, and the contact sheet — are licensed under the

**Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International Licence (CC-BY-4.0)**
<https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/>

They are original designs. Their forms follow long-standing geological
convention and were informed by the public-domain U.S. FGDC *Digital
Cartographic Standard for Geologic Map Symbolization* (FGDC-STD-013-2006, a
U.S. Government work in the public domain), but no FGDC artwork is copied into
this repository — the tiles are drawn from scratch by the generators in
`glacial_patterns/patterns.py`.

**Suggested citation when used in a figure:**
